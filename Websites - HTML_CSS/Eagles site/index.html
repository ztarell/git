New Faces in Old Places

After a dismal 2015 season (7-9) without a playoff berth, The Bird's fired their coach, Chip Kelly, and started new with Doug Pederson at the helm. Then, even more surprisingly, they traded a plethora of draft picks for the No. 2 spot to take an unknown but up and coming QB, Carson Wentz. But the big question is, will it payoff?





Was it Destiny?

The sudden injury for the Minnesota Vikings was all it took for The Eagles' starting veteran quarterback, Sam Bradford, to be traded and the unproven rookie, Carson Wentz jolted to the prestigious but unforgiving position. The hiring of defensive coordinator, Jim Schwartz, made the offseason acalades come full circle. Things might finally be looking bright for the city of brotherly love!
